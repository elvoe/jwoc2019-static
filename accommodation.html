Accommodation

Suggestions for accommodation in the Silkeborg/Aarhus area, suitable for training stay prior to or during JWOC 2019.

During the event we suggest that JWOC participants stay at the Event Centre and Tour participants sign up for the O-camp. The O-camp has been cancelled because of too few registrations.

On most websites you can choose other languages than Danish, primarily English. Look for the flags or the text "Sprog" (Language).
